About Us

Welcome to Do La Lash by Angela, a small local business located in the bustling city of Caloocan, Philippines. Our mission is to enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence through our precision and quality work. We specialize in eyelash extensions, offering a wide range of styles and lengths to suit your individual needs and preferences. Our experienced and skilled technicians use only the highest quality products to ensure that your lashes are not only beautiful, but also safe and healthy.



At Do La Lash by Angela, we believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best. That's why we offer affordable prices and flexible scheduling to accommodate the busy lifestyles of our clients. Our cozy salon is a sanctuary where you can relax and unwind while we work our magic on your lashes. We strive to create a friendly and welcoming environment where you can leave feeling confident and beautiful. Thank you for choosing Do La Lash by Angela, we look forward to helping you enhance your natural beauty.
